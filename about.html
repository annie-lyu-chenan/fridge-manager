About the Project

FridgeMate was created to help people stay connected to what's in their fridge. By organizing ingredients, tracking freshness, and offering simple nudges, it helps reduce food waste.

With FridgeMate, you can:

  • Log groceries by name, quantity, and expiration
  • Get weekly reminders for items expiring soon
  • Track your monthly food savings

Reducing waste starts with awareness. Small, mindful acts at home can lead to big changes for the planet.

About Me

Chenan (Annie) Lyu is a MS Marketing Communication Research student at Boston University College of Communication.

She is passionate about understanding people's behaviors and uncovering unspoken needs through thoughtful design. Inspired by real stories from roommates and friends struggling to manage what's in their fridge, Annie created this minimalistic tool to help people keep track of their food, reduce waste, and feel more in control of their everyday life :)
